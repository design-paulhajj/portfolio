001

Context

Healthdirect Australia, in partnership with the Department of Health, Disability and Ageing, set out to build a single, impartial digital tool to battle growing misinformation, particularly online, and help Australians make science-backed contraception decisions.

002

Opportunity

(Evidence led product design)

Build a research-driven UX strategy from scratch, running multi-audience sessions with consumers, GPs, and peak bodies to ground design in real-world needs.

(Safe, science backed source of truth)

A single, trusted resource designed with input from First Nations, LGBTIQ+ and CALD communities.

003

Outcomes

(Ethnographic engagement at scale)

Research program across consumers, GPs, peak bodies, and culturally diverse communities.

(Production ready prototype)

Production-ready interactive prototype incl. full decision-support flow, method content, and…

  6. 6 Product and tech hand-off and documentation
